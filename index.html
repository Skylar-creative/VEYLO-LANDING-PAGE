The Invisible Made Wearable

House of Sky is a philosophy of atmosphere. We believe that what surrounds you—air, light, scent—shapes your presence in the world.

VEYLO is our first expression: four fragrances that map to four altitudes, four moods, four ways of occupying space. Each scent is designed to be the first layer of your wardrobe, invisible yet essential.

In 2026, we extend into fabric. The same philosophy of breath and movement, now in garments.
